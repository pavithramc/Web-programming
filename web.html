ABOUT US

The College of Engineering Chennuer(CEC),was established by the govewrnment of kerala under the aegis of the IHRD,in the year 1993.It has been approved by All India Council for Technical Education and affiliated to APJ Abdul Kalam Technoligical University.Ever since its inception,the college has maintained its eminance asa leading engineering college in kerala.Being located in Chengannur town,Alleppy district the college has got access to all means of transport,commununication and lodging facilities.

COURSES OFFERED

Admission to the cources is on an annual basis and is based on all Kerala commom entrance examinations conducted by the Controller of Entrance Examinations,Government of Kerala.The admission to the free/merit seat and management seats are through the central alloutment process conducted by the controller of entrance examinations,Government of Kerala.
