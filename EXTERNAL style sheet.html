Trees are vital to the planet, providing essential resources like the oxygen we breathe, food, and materials such as wood and medicine. They are crucial for environmental health, as they absorb carbon dioxide, help regulate the climate, prevent soil erosion, and provide habitats for countless animals. Beyond their practical uses, trees also enhance the beauty of our world.
